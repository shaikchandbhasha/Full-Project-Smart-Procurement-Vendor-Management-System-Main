Smart Procurement Vendor Management System

This system is designed to manage vendor-related activities such as vendor registration, procurement processes, purchase orders, invoices, and payments.

Users of the system:
- Admin: Manages vendors and system settings
- Manager: Approves requests and monitors processes
- Employee: Creates requisitions and tracks orders
- Vendor: Uploads invoices and views purchase orders

The system improves efficiency, transparency, and reduces manual work in procurement.
